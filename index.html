Welcome to IEEE CS ISIMA SBC

The IEEE CS ISI Mahdia Chapter promotes computing technologies through events, workshops, and projects for students and professionals. Our goal is to advance the field of computing and provide valuable resources and networking opportunities for our members.

We organize various events such as seminars, workshops, and conferences to help students and professionals stay updated with the latest trends and technologies in Software Engineering, Cybersecurity, Cloud Computing, IoT, and other emerging computing technologies.

For more information, visit our Events page or contact us through our Contact page.

Our Mission

Our mission is to foster the development and application of computing technologies. We aim to provide a platform for students and professionals to collaborate, learn, and innovate in the field of computer science and related technologies.
